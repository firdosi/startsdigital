## Project Overview

Starts Digital has managed digital marketing, advertising creative, and e-commerce support for Black Gold Fertilizer, a Pakistan-wide lawn-care and organic fertilizer e-commerce brand, from 2024 to the present.

## High-Level Role & Services

- **Digital Strategy & Paid Social:** Structured Meta advertising campaigns targeting home gardening and lawn-care enthusiasts across Pakistan.
- **Creative Production:** Produced ad visual assets, scripts, and promotional graphics tailored to seasonal planting cycles.
- **E-Commerce & Conversion Support:** Supported WooCommerce storefront optimization, landing page performance, and analytics tracking.
- **Customer Retention & Digital Sales Support:** Assisted with digital customer communications and sales process optimization.

## Key Portfolio Metrics

- **PKR 30M+** in delivered-order revenue supported over 24 months.
- **29,000+** product sales supported across product categories.
- **22,000+** delivered sales nationwide.
