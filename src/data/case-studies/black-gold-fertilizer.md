## Partnership Overview

Starts Digital has worked with Black Gold Fertilizer since 2024 and continues to manage the brand's digital growth across Pakistan's consumer lawn and gardening market. The engagement covers customer acquisition, e-commerce, creative production, analytics, retention, sales support and order operations rather than advertising alone.

## Starting Point

Black Gold was a new business facing low sales, weak advertising, limited brand awareness, no properly optimised website, incomplete tracking and a high cost per order. Customers also needed education before they understood the product, how much to use and which supporting products could improve their lawn-care routine.

## Core Business Challenge

The long-term goal was to build a large base of returning customers instead of depending entirely on continuous paid acquisition. At the same time, the business had to manage seasonal demand, delivery charges, returned parcels, fake or low-quality orders, limited creative content and Meta acquisition costs that often increased when budgets were scaled.

## Full Digital Scope

Starts Digital and its team manage the complete digital side of the business. This includes Facebook and Instagram management, ad copywriting, video scripts, graphic direction, ad shoots, product photography, video editing, AI-assisted creative development, WordPress and WooCommerce development, landing pages, customer support, WhatsApp marketing, email and SMS communication, retargeting, reporting, team leadership, sales strategy, order handling and return analysis.

Product manufacturing, packing and physical shipping remain the client's responsibility. Digital ordering, customer communication, follow-up and return handling are managed by the Starts Digital team.

## Acquisition and Audience Strategy

The paid-media system combines Meta sales campaigns, Advantage+ Shopping, website conversion, WhatsApp, engagement, retargeting, lookalike and broad audience campaigns. Lookalike audiences performed best for website sales, while broad targeting worked effectively for engagement and awareness.

Budget decisions are controlled through rules that increase or decrease spend according to an affordable cost per result. This helped the team respond when Meta increased acquisition cost during scaling instead of allowing budgets to rise without commercial control.

## Product and Offer Optimisation

When the partnership began, the website promoted a 5kg fertilizer packet for PKR 1,450 as the primary offer. Starts Digital repositioned the main offer around a 7kg packet priced at PKR 1,950. The 7kg pack became the dominant order choice and improved profit per transaction.

The product range was also expanded beyond fertilizer to include Black Gold Defender, Black Gold Aerator and seed packs. This created more opportunities to cross-sell useful products instead of relying on a single-item business model.

## Website and Checkout Improvements

The website journey was simplified by removing unnecessary checkout fields and automatically redirecting customers to the cart after adding a product. CAPTCHA protection was added to reduce fake orders.

Tracking was rebuilt across Meta Pixel, Conversions API, Google Analytics 4, Google Tag Manager, purchase events, WhatsApp and TikTok. These changes created a clearer view of customer actions and campaign performance than the original setup provided.

## Sales-Team Enablement and Real Order Value

Website acquisition sometimes appeared expensive, and Meta could report a return on ad spend below 2. However, the initial website basket did not represent the customer's final purchase value.

Call representatives were trained to recommend packet quantity according to lawn size and to introduce relevant supporting products. As a result, many website customers increased their final order to approximately two or three times the value originally placed online. Campaign decisions therefore considered delivered-order economics, call outcomes and final order value rather than relying only on platform-reported ROAS.

## Creative and Content System

The strongest creative approaches included before-and-after lawn results, product demonstrations, customer-problem angles, lawn-improvement education, the refund promise, seasonal offers, influencer content and customer testimonials.

Starts Digital manages the full creative workflow across ideas, scripts, copy, production, editing, AI-assisted advertising and testing. The team also represented the brand at the CHAICON exhibition as part of its wider awareness and customer-acquisition activity.

## Retention and Customer Reactivation

Post-delivery follow-up was introduced to build trust, support repeat purchases and maintain contact with customers after the first order. Black Gold now has more than 20,000 previous customers available for retention outreach.

Direct outreach to existing customers currently supports sales of more than 50 packets per day. Because this retention system can generate recurring orders, current paid spend can remain limited or focus mainly on awareness and incremental acquisition instead of carrying the entire sales operation.

## Order, Delivery and Return Operations

A structured parcel follow-up system was created to reduce avoidable returns and maintain customer communication throughout delivery. The team monitors orders, follows up after dispatch, handles return-related communication and uses delivery outcomes when evaluating campaign profitability.

This operational view is important because a campaign can appear successful inside an advertising platform while losing money through failed delivery, returns or weak customer handling.

## Commercial Results

Over a 24-month period, the partnership supported:

- PKR 30M+ in revenue from delivered orders.
- 29,000+ product sales, including more than 22,000 delivered sales.
- A previous-customer base of more than 20,000 buyers available for retention activity.
- More than 50 packet sales per day currently supported through existing-customer outreach.
- An active multi-product e-commerce model covering fertilizer, Defender, Aerator and seed products.

These results reflect a combined system of paid acquisition, website optimisation, customer support, upselling, retention, delivery follow-up and ongoing campaign management.

## Key Learning

Platform ROAS alone does not always show the complete commercial result. When trained representatives increase order value after checkout, the business must evaluate final confirmed value, delivered revenue, return behaviour and repeat purchases alongside advertising-platform data.

The Black Gold partnership demonstrates how acquisition, retention, website conversion, sales-team training and delivery operations can work together to build a stronger e-commerce business than advertising alone.
